Under the Disability Discrimination Act 1992, goods, services and information provided by any business have to be provided in a non-discriminatory accessible manner.

This website has been adapted to meet the legal standard established in respect of this requirement.

This site is currently compliant to Level A of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines version 2.0(WCAG2.0) standard.

WCAG 2.0 is a technical standard developed under the Web Accessibility Initiative of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).
